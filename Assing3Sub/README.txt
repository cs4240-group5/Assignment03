Game is developed for HTC Vive Headset. 

Open Zoo Scene in the Scenes folder and click play.

Controls
Teleport - Left trigger Button, press trigger to aim, release trigger to teleport
Grab and Shoot - Right trigger button, press trigger to grab, release to shoot. No cross hair/laser provided to make the game harder.

Instructions:
Pick up a food from the table, and throw it towards the animals to feed them. Feed them the correct items:

Rabiits eat carrot. Bears eat fish.
Tigers eat chicken. Birds eat bread. 

Score will be updated when you successfully feed the animal.
